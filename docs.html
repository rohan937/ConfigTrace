Teams & workspaces

Every ConfigTrace account belongs to a workspace. A workspace holds your integrations, your team's timeline view, and your billing plan.

  • →Roles: Owner (full control), Admin (manage integrations and members), Member (read-only timeline).
  • →Inviting teammates: Go to Settings → Workspace → Members. Enter an email and assign a role. The invitee receives a link; if the email doesn't arrive, share the fallback invite link directly.
  • →Workspace switcher: You can belong to multiple workspaces. Use the workspace switcher in the sidebar to move between them.
  • →Audit log: All admin actions (invites, integration changes, billing events) are recorded at Settings → Workspace → Audit Log.

Billing & limits

ConfigTrace offers three plans. Only workspace owners and admins can manage billing.

Plan Integrations Members Sync interval History
Free 3 1 (owner) 60 min 30 days
Pro $29/mo 20 5 15 min 180 days
Team $79/mo 100 25 5 min 365 days

Billing is managed at Settings → Workspace → Billing. If you hit an integration or member limit, ConfigTrace will block the action and show an upgrade prompt.
